27Balloons

This is a software art birthday present for my friend Mike Knapp in San Francisco. The jelly physics bubbles react to the mouse clicks, and overlap like stained glass. I did the piece during a time when I was obsessed with balloons. Press the 's' key to render one still frame to disk as a PNG. Shout into your microphone to disturb the bubbles.
