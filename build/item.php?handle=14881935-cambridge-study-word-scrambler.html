Cambridge Study Word Scrambler

One season, I was getting this ridiculous email forward about the readability of messed up words. After the 3rd time I received the forward, I decided to make an online application that would scramble peoples' text for them. The application caught a meme wave, and people started emailing me thinking I was the authoritative "Cambridge" researcher who originated the "study."
