Creative VECTOR tool re-writes

Vector - A series of creative variations on a vector path tool, commonly found in vector graphics software like Illustrator and Freehand. Vertices are allowed to behave in ways which celebrate the act of drawing a shape by laying down points. These are part of a larger series, in which I explored making different post-tool, and post-video-game art.

These sketches were done during an independent study session with artist Rebecca Allen.
