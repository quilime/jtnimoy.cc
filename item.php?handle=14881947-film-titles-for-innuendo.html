Film Titles for Innuendo

Innuendo is a film produced by Dayna Frank and written and directed by Ethan Tobman, alumni of the NYU Tisch film school. I was asked to do the titles and credits for this spoof comedy Sundance entry. The below image is the concept tests. The final version turned out to be much less ornimented. The idea was to subtley mimic the old news flashes from noir films; most notibly in Citizen Kane.
